Privacy policy

How this site handles data.

This policy applies to the Debate Card Cutter website, its API endpoints, and related export features served from this site.

Effective date: April 24, 2026

1. What this service does

This service helps users research, extract, format, and export debate cards. It may accept claims, source URLs, snippets, pasted text, formatted cards, and export requests in order to generate debate-ready outputs.

2. Information this service may receive

Depending on how you use the site or API, the service may receive:

  • source URLs that you provide or that are selected for research
  • snippets used to locate a passage within a source
  • claim text, draft tags, and card text
  • source metadata such as title, author, publication, and date
  • API request metadata and server logs
  • generated export files such as `.docx` documents

3. How information is used

Information submitted to the service may be used to:

  • fetch and parse webpages or documents
  • extract passages between provided snippets
  • format debate cards and citations
  • generate downloadable export files
  • debug, monitor, and improve service reliability

4. Logs and local storage

This server may store operational logs, including request information, research activity, provider activity, and error logs, on the host machine. The site interface may also use browser local storage to keep saved cards on your device.

5. Exported files

When you request a Word export, the server may generate and store a `.docx` file in a server-side downloads directory so it can be retrieved through a download link. These files may remain on the server until they are manually removed.

6. Third-party requests

When this service fetches source material, it may connect to third-party websites that host the source content. If AI providers or other external APIs are configured on the server, claim text, source text, and related content may also be sent to those providers in order to complete the requested task.

7. Sharing of information

This service does not sell personal information. Information may be disclosed to third-party websites or service providers only as needed to retrieve sources, run configured model providers, or deliver requested functionality.

8. Retention

Logs, generated files, and other server-side artifacts may be retained until they are rotated, deleted, or otherwise removed by the site operator. Because this service is self-hosted, retention practices may change as the operator updates the deployment.

9. Security

The operator may take reasonable steps to secure this service, but no internet-connected system can be guaranteed fully secure. Do not submit information you would not want transmitted to third-party websites, external model providers, or stored in server logs.

10. Your choices

You can avoid sending certain information by not submitting it to the site or API. If you do not want a document stored for download, do not use the export feature. You can also avoid browser local storage by not saving cards in the site interface.

11. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date when changes are made.

12. Contact

For privacy questions about this deployment, contact the operator of this site through the contact information provided wherever this service is published or shared.
